Our Craft

Precision in Every Detail

At FORMA, we believe that comfort and beauty are not mutually exclusive. Each chair goes through a 47-step quality inspection process before leaving our facility, ensuring that what arrives at your door is nothing short of perfection.

Our proprietary AirFlow Mesh technology provides superior breathability compared to traditional foam cushions, maintaining ideal spinal alignment while keeping you cool during even the most intense work sessions.

  • Cold-forged aircraft-grade aluminum base
  • AirFlow Mesh with 360° breathability
  • Memory foam lumbar cushion — adjustable in 3 dimensions
  • Precision ball-bearing tilt mechanism with 15° lock range
  • PU-coated smooth-glide casters for all floor types
